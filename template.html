Welcome!

The Community Support Tracker is a project by PiXELL River Financial, developed to monitor and manage community support initiatives. The tool helps track donations, log volunteer hours, and manage event signups, ensuring a meaningful impact in the communities we serve. The project is divided into three main components:

  • Donation Tracker: Manage and track charitable contributions (Developed by Maulik).
  • Volunteer Hours Tracker: Log and monitor volunteer efforts (Developed by Om).
  • Event Signup: Organize and manage participation in community events (Developed by Rushil).
Together, these tools promote PiXELL River Financial's community engagement, fostering a culture of giving and collaboration within the company.
